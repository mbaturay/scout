TITLE
Football Match Analytics Engine (Single Broadcast Video → Advanced Stats)

VERSION
1.0.0 (MVP)

PRODUCT DEFINITION (WHAT WE ARE BUILDING)
A local-first, free, open-source football analytics engine that ingests a full match video recording (broadcast or single-camera wide angle) and outputs:

1. structured tracking data (players + ball) mapped to pitch coordinates,
2. a curated set of advanced match statistics and tactical insights,
3. an annotated video, and
4. machine-readable exports (JSON/CSV/Parquet) for custom analysis.

PRIMARY USERS

* Analysts / hobbyists / coaches who have a full-match video and want advanced insights without manual tagging.
* Developers who want a stats-ready dataset for their own models and dashboards.

GOALS (WHAT “AMAZING STATS” MEANS IN MVP)
The MVP must produce, at minimum, these stats from a full match video:

A) Physical & Movement

* Estimated distance covered per player (pitch-mapped)
* Sprint counts (threshold-based) + top speed
* Team tempo: median ball speed, median player speed by team

B) Spatial & Tactical Shape

* Team centroid over time (attack/defense phases)
* Team width/length and compactness (per minute + rolling window)
* Defensive line height estimate (per team, per window)

C) Ball Progression & Territory

* Ball progression rate (m/s toward goal)
* Time in thirds (defensive/middle/attacking third)
* Possession proxy (time ball is nearest to team / controlled zone heuristic)

D) Pressure & Control (proxy models, no event labels needed)

* Pitch control (Voronoi-based) per frame and team control ratio
* Pressure index on ball carrier: nearby opponents within radius + closing speed

E) Threat / Danger

* xT (expected threat) from ball movement using an open xT grid
* Threat by zone occupancy (zone-based heuristics)

OUTPUTS MUST BE:

* Per-frame time series
* Per-player aggregates (match totals)
* Per-team aggregates (match totals)
* Optional rolling-window summaries

NON-GOALS (OUT OF SCOPE FOR MVP)

* Perfect event detection with ground-truth accuracy
* Multi-camera stitching
* Player identity / jersey number OCR
* Real-time live processing

INPUTS & ASSUMPTIONS
Supported video types:

* Full match recording, ideally 720p+
* Broadcast feed OR single static wide-angle camera
* Variable frame rate allowed

Known hard problems (must handle gracefully):

* Camera cuts/replays/closeups
* Motion blur and occlusions
* Ball missing intermittently
* Pitch keypoints not visible in some frames

The system must:

* Detect non-play frames and mark them
* Continue tracking across short occlusions
* Provide confidence scores for analytics reliability

SUCCESS METRICS (ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA)
A run is considered successful if:

Pipeline

* CLI completes without crash on full match video
* Produces annotated video + exports + summary report
* Exports are schema-stable

Tracking/Mapping Quality

* Homography available for ≥60% of in-play frames
* Ball positions available for ≥40% of in-play frames
* Player tracks stable enough for rolling movement stats

Stats Quality

* All MVP stats computed and included in outputs
* Each stat includes confidence indicator

SYSTEM CONSTRAINTS

* Free/open-source dependencies only
* No paid APIs
* Runs locally on CPU; GPU optional
* Windows supported

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

FR1 — Video Ingestion

* Load video, extract fps, frame count, resolution
* Generate frames with configurable stride
* Output metadata.json

FR2 — In-play Filter

* Classify frames as IN_PLAY vs NOT_IN_PLAY
* Store frame flags

FR3 — Object Detection

* Detect player, goalkeeper, referee, ball
* Load weights locally

FR4 — Tracking

* Assign persistent track IDs
* Store tracking confidence metrics

FR5 — Team Classification

* Assign team_id ∈ {0,1}
* Provide clustering confidence
* Goalkeeper resolution heuristic

FR6 — Keypoints & Homography

* Detect 32 pitch landmarks
* Compute homography + quality score
* Expose frame↔pitch transforms

FR7 — Pitch-Mapped Positions

* Convert positions to pitch coordinates
* Smooth ball trajectory
* Interpolate short gaps

FR8 — Analytics (MVP Stats)

* Implement all stats in GOALS A–E
* Output per-frame + aggregates + confidence

FR9 — Visualization

* Annotated video with IDs, team colors, ball marker
* Optional radar overlay

FR10 — Exports
Write to out/:

* frames.jsonl or Parquet
* players_summary.csv
* teams_summary.csv
* stats folder
* run_report.json (coverage + quality metrics)

FR11 — CLI
python -m football_ai_pipeline --config configs/default.yaml --input <video.mp4> --output <out_dir>

DATA MODEL (SCHEMA REQUIREMENTS)
FrameState minimum fields:

* frame_idx, timestamp_sec
* flags {in_play, reasons}
* detections
* tracks
* team_assignments
* keypoints + confidences
* homography + quality
* pitch positions + quality
* ball_pitch + quality
